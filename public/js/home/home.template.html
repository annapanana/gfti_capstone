how does it work?

Step 1: Theme Selection

We have carefully crafted 4 different themes to choose from. Each theme includes different customization assets that will give a different visual identity to your postcard. The themes include: Classic, Playful, Modern and Wild

Step 2: Photo Upload

If you are using this site on a mobile device, you can take a photo and directly upload it to the site. Alternatively, you can upload a photo from your desktop or provide an image URL. The postcard layout is optomized for mobile uploads.

Step 3: Edit Design

You can use 5 different widgets to customize your design. Tools include image filters, border themes, color selection, font selection and subtext customization. The design assets and colors are specific to the theme that you select.

Step 4: Message & Address

Fill in a custom message and address the card for mailing. Your desing will be saved to the postcard archive for sharing with the community but your message and addresses will not be saved in order to respect your privacy.

Step 5: Pay and Mail

When you have completed your postcard, you can mail it. You will recieve an estimated delivery date. Each postcard costs $1.50 to send, which includes printing and postage. We use Stripe for secure payments.
